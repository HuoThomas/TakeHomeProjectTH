Sweet Spicy Saint Louis Spare Ribs

Micah Zakahi | micah.zakahi@siemens.com | Updated Jun 1, 2020
ribs apple pecan pork
Edit

Description

Saint Louis cut spare ribs slow smoked with Pecan and Apple Wood.

Directions

  1. Remove ribs from package and pat dry
  2. Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs
  3. Apply sweet spicy dry rub generously to all sides and put back in fridge for 1-4 hours
  4. Preheat smoker to 225
  5. Smoke ribs for 3 hours and remove ribs when the meat is starting to pull back from the bone
  6. Add brown sugar and honey to one side of the ribs and wrap in aluminum foil
  7. Place back on smoker for about 2 hours
  8. Remove from foil and sauce with favorite bbq sauce
  9. Put back on smoker and allow sauce to thicken on ribs
  10. Probe meat after 30 min, consistency should feel like going through butter
  11. Pull from smoker and let them rest for at least 15 min
  12. Cut and Enjoy
